Archery calculators and honest gear reviews. From someone who shoots 3D every other weekend, not a YouTube influencer with a sponsorship deck.

Archery's hard. The math at the start of the hobby is where most people get burned, buying a bow that's too heavy, arrows that are too stiff, or a sight that costs more than the bow. The shops would rather not explain it because the explanation costs them a sale.

These tools are the math. Free, no signup, no email capture. If you bookmark one and use it twice a year before tournament season, that's the goal.
